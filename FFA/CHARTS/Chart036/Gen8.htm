Also living with this family in the 1860 census of Harrison Co., KY were William C. French, age 23; Anna Painter, age 4; and Lawson Taylor, age 68. Their father, Charles, was a farmer, and William C. French was a laborer.  This French family and his brother Hugh, above, were the only French families living in Harrison Co., KY, in 1860. By 1870, they had all moved out of Harrison Co., KY. Both Hugh and his brother Charles moved to Robertson Co., KY. Hugh was living in Pinhook, and Charles was living in Kentontown.
