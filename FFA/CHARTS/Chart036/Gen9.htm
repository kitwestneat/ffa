Leslie Ray French Jr., 47, of 10605 Timberlake Road, owner of French's Market for the past 17 years, died today at 4:40 a.m. at his home after a long illness. Born in Covington, he was the son of the of Mrs. Lovelene Vaughan French and the late Leslie Ray French. A veteran of World War II, he was a member of Timberlake Methodist Church. In addition to his mother and wife, Mrs. Juanita Williams French, he is survived by two sons, Leslie R. French III and Dan E. French, both of Lynchburg, two brothers, Linwood J. French of Richmond and Thomas B. French of Burney, Calif. Whitten Timberlake Chapel is in charge of funeral arrangements which will be announced later. Transcribed 11 Jul 2006 - Michael Vance Baker, Obit provided be by Dan E. French.
