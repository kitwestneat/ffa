Index Basic

Robert French (1), b. ca. 1775 in NJ, m. Rachel Manning who was b. in CT; Phillip (2), b. ca. 1804 in NY, m1. Amanda Barber, m2. Julia Caroline Walker; Evangeline Harriet French (3), b. 17 Mar 1874 in Washington Heights, IL, m. Frank A. Catchpool; Eldora H. Catchpool (4), m. Lora Paul Prickett; Jacquelin Ann Prickett (5), m. Jack Rieke; Jan Rieke (6). Phillip (2) also had a son, Robert T. French, who was the founder of French's Mustard.
