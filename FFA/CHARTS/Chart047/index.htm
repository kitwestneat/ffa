History and Research

The family lived in Halifax County, VA; Rowan County, NC; and in 1753 they lived in Orange County, NC; in 1785 in Wentworth City, Rockingham County, NC (which formerly was Orange County). Rockingham Co. formed in 1785 from Guilford County. Recording of wills began in 1804. Old unrecorded wills have been found in courthouses.

First Generation

1.1* William French Sr., b. ca. 1770 in Halifax Co., VA, living in Rockingham Co., NC in 1790 [3].

1.2 Jeremiah French, b. ca. 1772, in Halifax Co., VA.

Second Generation

Children of William French, Sr., 1.1

2.1 William French Jr., b. before 1790 in Rockingham Co., NC [3].

2.2 Mason French of Rockingham Co. in 1790 [3].

2.3* Henry C. French, b. Apr 1795 in VA as per the 1860 census when Henry was 65 years old living in Benton, Holt Co., Missouri [9]. At that time they were living with the Glick family on a farm, and Henry was a farmer. He m. Catherine (or Katherine) Shultz on 1 Feb 1830 in Morgan, Indiana [9]. He d. in 1869 in Holt, Missouri [9]. They had 5 children, all born in Indiana. The family ended up in Holt Co., MO around 1853 [2]. Catherine was b. 1803 (she was 57 in the 1860 census) in Rowan Co., NC, the dau. of Christian Schaltz and Elizabeth Goss [9]. She d. in 1861 in Holt, Missouri [9].

2.4 Samuel French, b. ca. 1796/97 in VA, m. Jane Hollingsworth on 20 Dec 1827 in Morgan Co., IN [2]. She was b. in 1805 [9].

Third Generation

Children of Henry C. and Catherine (Shultz) French, 2.3

3.1 Martina/Martella/Martilla A. French, b. 1 Jan 1833 (as per the 1860 census) in Indiana [2], d. 25 aug 1911 in Holt, Missouri. She m. Christopher Columbus Strong on 4 Jan 1866 in Holt, Missouri and had 2 children [9].

3.2 Andrew Jackson French (Jack), b. 1837 in Indiana [2].

3.3* Samson/Sampson French, b. May 1837 in Morgan, Indiana [2]. He m. Julia Ann Blackmore on 25 Nov 1866 in Holt, Missouri [9]. She was b. 4 Aug 1878.

3.4 James French, b. 1840 in Indiana [2].

3.5 Neta French, b. 1 Jul 1841 in Indiana [2], d. 26 Jan 1923 and is buried at the Tharp Cemetery in Holt Co., Missouri [9]. Neta m. William S. Dunnihoo in 1858 and had 4 children: William, Hiram, Wheeler, and Robert C. [9]. She d. 26 Jan 1923 in Missouri City, Fort Bend, TX [9].

Children of Samuel and Jane (Hollingsworth) French, 2.4

3.6 Mastely French, b. ca. 1830 in Cloverdale, Putnam Co., IN and moved to TX [2].

3.7 Louisa French, b. ca. 1833 in IN and moved to TX [2]. She m. Levi L. Fowler on 8 Jul 1853 in Owen Co., IN. They had son John S. Fowler.

3.8* Sanders French, b. 1834 in Putnam, IN and moved to TX [2]. He m. Roxina Keeling on 30 Dec 1852 in Owen, IN. She was b. 1836 in Owen, IN, and d. 1906 in Indiana Territory, OK. They had one son, James, b. in 1855 in Collin, TX [9]. Sanders was Roxanna Keeling’s first husband and their son James French was raised by Roxanne’s 2nd husband, Wilson Howard. He married in Collins Co Texas and went over to Oklahoma with his step-sister Palmira Howard and her husband John Sinclair Fowler.   Annie Fowler Musler and James French's daughter Annie were good friends. The Fowler family was also close to the Samuel French family and remembers their son Pervin French as a schoolmate and friend.   Roxanna’s sisters Palmira and Rosa J. Keeling also married Sanders brothers Marion and Wm R. French.

3.9 Sarah M. French, b. ca. 1838 in Cloverdale, Putnam Co., IN and moved to TX [2].

3.10 Francis Marion French (male) , b. in Cloverdale, Putnam Co., IN and moved to TX [2]. He m. Palmira Keeling on 30 Jun 1856 in Collins Co., TX.

3.11 John French, b. ca. 1840 in Cloverdale, Putnam Co., IN and moved to TX [2].

3.12 William Randolph French, b. ca. 1841 in Cloverdale, Putnam Co., IN and moved to TX [2]. He m. Rosa J. Keeling on 28 Mar 1860 in Collins Co., TX

3.13 Ellen Penchy French, b. ca. 1843 in Cloverdale, Putnam Co., IN and moved to TX [2]. She m. Abner Franklin on 9 Jan 1860 in Collins Co., TX.

Connection to the Wall Family of Rockingham Co., NC

David P. French, son-in-law, was executor of Daniel Wall’s estate, 25 Jan 1846 in Rockingham Co., NC. Rachel C. Wall, b. 1829, m. 5 Nov 1845 to David Porter French before 1876 in Rockingham, NC. David was b. ca. 1823 [1]. David was 27 in the 1850 census, Rachel was 21 [10]. Their children from the 1850 Census:

E. J. French, b. 1848 female, in Rockingham Co., NC, was 2 years old at census [10]. R. A. French, b. 1849 female, in Rockingham Co., NC, was 1 year old at census  [10]. S. E. French, b. 1850 in Rockingham Co., NC, was 4 months and 12 days old at census [10].

Anna C. Wall, b. 1820, m. 14 Nov 1846 to John H. French in Rockingham, NC [1]. John was b. in 1820 as he was 30 in the 1850 census, as was his wife [10]. Their children from the 1850 Census:

William French, b. 1848 in Rockingham Co., NC, age 22 months in 1850 census [10]. J. D. French, b. 1850 in Rockingham Co., NC, was 8 months, 12 days old in 1850 census [10].
