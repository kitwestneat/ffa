Immigration 1727-1740

The major immigrants from a list of more than tens of thousands of Germans connected with the French family of FFA Chart #195 for reasons of owning land nearby, being naturalized at the same time George French was naturalized in Annapolis, MD, in 1747, or for marrying into the French family, are stated below.

      Peter Rule, ship Adventure Galley, Rotterdam, Oct 1727 (Rule family connected with Peter French of FFA Chart #129)

      Jacob Miller, ship Alexander and Anne, 5 Sep 1730 (Miller married French)

      Jacob Snevely, ship Snow Lowther, Rotterdam, 14 Oct 1731 (Snevely married French)

      Henrick Ebby, ship Samuel, 11 Aug 1732 (Henrick Ebby or Henry Avey was naturalized with George French in 1747)

      Lisbetha, Kathrina, John, and Mergretha Evy, ship Samuel, 11 Aug 1732 (children of Henry Avey; Lisbetha Avey may have married George French)

      Jacob Miller, ship Johnson Galley, 18 Sep 1732 (Miller married French)

      Henrick Miller, ship Johnson Galley, 18 Sep 1732 (Miller married French)

      Johan Jacob Felker, age 17, ship Pink Plaisance, 21 Sep 1732 (Felker married French)

      Hans, age 37, and Elisbat Snebliey, age 44, Barbra Snebliey, age 24, Hanes Snebliey, age 11, Kasper Snebliey, age 9, Katrina Snebliey, age 6, Anna Katrina Snebliey, age 12, Anna Barbra Shebliey, age 10, ship Hope, 28 Aug 1733 (French married Snebliey)

      Christian Sheybly, age 53, and Christian Sheibly, age 17, ship Harle, 1 Sep 1736

      Joseph Wohlgemuth, ship Harle, 1 Sep 1736

      *Jacob Frantz, ship Robert and Alice, 11 Sep 1738, did take and subscribe the Oaths of the Govt.

      *Jacob Frank, age 30, ship Nancy, 20 Sep 1738

      *Jacob Frans, age 34, ship Elizabeth, 30 Oct 1738

      *Jacob Frantz, ship Elizabeth, 30 Oct 1738

      Peter Rule or Ruhl, ship Samuel and Snow Betsie, 27 Aug 1739 (French married Rule)

      *Jacob Frank, age 25, ship Friendship, 3 Sep 1739

      *Johann Jacob Franck, ship Friendship, 3 Sep 1739

      Jacob Wagner, age 20, ship Samuel & Elizabeth, 30 Sep 1740

      Henry and Johannes Wagner, ship Loyal Judith, 25 Nov 1740

*Discounted as being French as these names are viable names in the new colonies and were not altered to French, plus their DNA does not match with French.
