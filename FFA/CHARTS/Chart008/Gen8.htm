Eighth Generation

Children of John Francis French and Abby Starr Mercer, 7.4

8.1 Mary Blodgett French, b. 6 Dec 1852 [61]. She attended her brother James’ wedding on 18 Oct 1893; she was single.

8.2 John Carlos (or Charles) French, b. 1854, m. Isabell Hyland of Brooklyn, NY [61], and had daughter Isabel French, b. ca. 1886.

8.3 Frank M. French, b. 12 Feb 1856 [61] in New York.

8.4* James Morgan French, b. 6 Oct 1859 in CT, d. 11 Jul 1921 [61]. He appears in the 1900 census of Brooklyn, Kings Co., NY, as age 40, married to Maria French, in which he states that both his parents were born in CT; he is living with children Maria (5), James (4), John (2). He appears in the 1910 census of Brooklyn, Kings Co., NY, as age 50, married to Marie French, age 42, with children James (14), John (12), Abram (2), and Marie (15). He appears in the 1920 census of Westwood, Bergen Co., NJ, with his wife Maria and children James (23), John (21), and Abram (12). He died the next year.

Marriage

James m. Maria Gill, dau. of Dr. Charles R. Gill and Delores Ponce de Leon, of Cuba, on 18 Oct 1893, by Ref. Charles Hall of the Holy Trinity Church in Brooklyn, NY [61]. The article below is from The New York Times, October 19, 1893.

The article below is from The New York Times, 22 Oct 1893.

The article below is from The New York Times, July 14, 1891.

Children of David and Amelia (Guedry) French, 7.9

8.5 Mary Electa French, b. 14 Jul 1851 in Beaumont, TX, m. 22 Dec 1870 to Thomas H. Langham, d. 17 Aug 1934 in Beaumont, TX [19]. Thomas was b. 3 Aug 1846, d. 19 Apr 1920 in Beaumont, TX, the son of James “Bid” and Sarah Langham [19]. Thomas Langham was Sheriff of Jefferson County, TX for 24 years and Mayor of Beaumont for 4 years. He retired from both positions. He acquired a great deal of property and became very wealthy [19] [46]. See Chapter 4, Related Families, for Mary Electa French’s related families Langham, Treadaway, Wier, Junker, and Spencer.

8.6 Cornelia French I, b. 23 Nov 1853 in Beaumont, TX, d. 1 Dec 1853 in Beaumont, TX [9] [19].

8.7 Cornelia Clarinda/Clarenda French, b. 17 Feb 1855 [19] in Beaumont, TX, m1. 15 Oct 1873 to Nicholas Blanchette, m2. August DeLaune 13 Nov 1900 [19]. This was August’s second marriage – his first marriage was to Cornelia’s sister, Azema, who died. Cornelia d. 10 Jul 1930 in Beaumont. Nicholas d. 19 Jan 1900. August was b. 21 Jul 1854 and d. 23 Oct 1935. See Chapter 4, Related Families, for Cornelia French’s related family Blanchette.

8.8* John Erasmus French, b. 20 Dec 1856 in Beaumont, TX, m. 15 Sep 1883 to Emma Olive Pivoto/Peveto, dau. of Joseph and Adeline (Wright) Pivoto, d. 29 Aug 1934 in Beaumont, TX. Emma was b. 2 Jul 1864 in Nome, TX, d. 10 May 1947 in Beaumont, TX [7] [19], buried at the Magnolia Cemetery in Beaumont, TX. Erasmus was called Erastus by some of his family as a joke.

8.9 David Richard French, Jr., b. 25 Feb 1859 in Beaumont, TX, m. 1 Nov 1883 to Nora Alypha West, d. 23 Apr 1904 in Beaumont, TX. Nora was b. 26 Jun 1863, d. 21 Jul 1889 in childbirth along with the infant [19].

8.10 Azema/Azama Virginia French, b. 28 Mar 1865 in Beaumont, TX, m. 20 May 1884 to August DeLaune. She d. 11 Aug 1894. August was b. 21 Jul 1854, d. 23 Oct 1935. After Azema’s death, August married her sister, Cornelia [19]. Azema French’s related families: DeLaune, Maley, Morris, and Ewing. Azema had daughter Bertha DeLaune who m. John Henry Maley of Kansas City, MO [63]. John Henry and Bertha Maley had dau. Virginia Maley who m. Robert J. Morris, Jr., D.D.S. of New Orleans, and had 8 children including Tommy Morris [63].

8.11 Andrew Jackson French, b. 30 Mar 1868 in Beaumont, TX, did not marry, d. 11 Dec 1891 [19].

8.12 Theodore Alwyn French, b. 19 Nov 1869 in Beaumont, TX, m. 3 wives, no issue, d. 20 Mar 1932 in Beaumont, TX [19]. Theodore m1. Minnie A. LaBelle in 1893. She was known as Minnie Bee Justus. Theodore was Minnie’s aunt’s husband. When her aunt died, Minnie’s mother m. Theodore. Minnie Bee Justus’ father was both her aunt’s husband and her mother’s husband. (Told to me by the elderly lady living in David French’s house next to the French Trading Post in 1988). Theodore m2. Iola Edwards, m3. Edna Justice.

8.13* William (Willie) Lee French, b. 3 Apr 1872 [19] or 1878 in Beaumont, TX, m. 27 Feb 1895 to Ellen Langham, d. 6 Jul 1907 in Beaumont, TX. [19].

8.14 Sibyl (Sibbie) Selesta/Celesta French, b. 28 Dec 1878 in Beaumont, TX, m1. Ralph Orr on 17 Feb 1900, m2. Asa Bordages, d. 1963. See Chapter 4, Related Families, for Sibyl French’s related family Orr.
