I am 49 years old, and all my life I have had an interest in the French family name. My grandfather instilled this in me when I was very young. I have information leading back to William and Halsted, his son (perhaps nephew) Francis and so on. My line goes form Francis to Francis Jr... Samuel... Noah... Francis... John... James Morgan... Abram Gill... Gregory Morgan... and then to me. I find this all very exciting, and in addition to much of the stories surrounding some of these characters, I have learned some very interesting facts surrounding relatives along the way. I have a son, who coincidently will be attending a school in Cambridge, MA. come September. Actually, he is already there for the summer doing a special academic program. The school is MIT, and is just south of where it all started for the French family in New England. I am planning to be in that area at the end of the month. Might you know of any French's, active in the study of our history and related to William, that may still exist in the area of Billerica? If I could connect with someone there, it would be like a dream come true for me. I would like to visit grave sites as well, and any other land marks that might stir my imagination for centuries gone by. If you have any suggestions, please do pass them on to me.
