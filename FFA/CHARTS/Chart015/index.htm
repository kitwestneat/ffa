History and Research

Mostly likely this chart (and FFA Chart #154) tie into FFA Chart #3 from Taunton, MA. See Ref [4].

First Generation

1.1* William French, b. ca. 1768 in Taunton, MA, m. Bethia Porter [5] in Norfolk, Litchfield Co., CT.

Second Generation

Children of William and Bethia (Porter) French, 1.1

2.1 Sibel (or Sybel or Sybil) Martha French, b. 28 Jan 1796 in Bristol, Norfolk Co., MA [5] [4], m. William Palmer Judd ca. 1818, d. between 1860 and 1870. All Sybil Judds children were born in Norfolk Co., CT: Mary (1818), Caroline (1820), Catherine (1822), Henry (1824), Walter (1826), Edwin (1828), James (1832), Frederick (1834), Frances Arabella (1837), Margaret Orianna (1838), Frank H. (1844). In 1850 Henry Judd was living in Prospect, New Haven Co., CT.

2.2 Lephia (or Lepha) French, m. Spencer Pease [1], intention of marriage 20 Dec 1840 in Washington, MA. On the death certificate for Lephia Pease it lists her parents as William French and Bethia (or Bethiah) Porter, both born in Taunton, MA [1].

2.3 Polly Mariah French.

2.4 Bethia Eliza French.
