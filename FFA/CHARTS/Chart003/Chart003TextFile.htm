Second Generation

5.      Joseph French:: (1.John1) b. 04 Apr 1640, Probably Cambridge, MA, m. 04 Nov 1663, in Billerica, MA, Experience Foster:, b. about 1644,5 (daughter of Thomas Foster: and Elizabeth) d. after 1720.  Joseph died 1694, Taunton, MA.

        Joseph moved between 1678 and 1680  to the southern part Taunton, which became  Berkley in 1735, and took land next to James Phillips, Richard Burt and Elkanah Babbit. Here his last four children were born.  His will dated 24 April 1694, mentions the following children: Joseph, John, Nathaniel, Jacob, Ebenezer, Jonathan, Elizabeth, wife of Jas. Phillips; his wife, Experience, was the executrix of the will.

        Joseph saw active service in King Phillip's War in 1675, was a member of the 4th Sq. 1st Military Co. of Taunton,  in 1682, and was listed in the inspection return of King William's War of 1691.
