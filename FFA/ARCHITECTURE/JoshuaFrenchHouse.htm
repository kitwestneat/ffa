Joshua French is a grandson of Samuel French the Joiner, FFA Chart #11. 
This house is still standing at 671 West Road, Manchester, Vermont,
and last sold in 2015.

This house sold for $680,000 on 29 July 2015. It is a single-family home with 5 beds, 3 full baths, 1 partial bath, 4,300 sq. ft. on 3.8 acres. RENOVATED Stately 18th century center hall colonial, fabulous new kitchen with exposed antique beams and cathedral ceiling, back stair case, new stone patio, stone walls and spring fed pools-6 Rumford fireplaces bring warmth to spacious sunlit rooms-Fabulous proportions & period architectural details throughout. Guest cottage, workshop, studio, garden shed, spring house. Historic Equinox Mtn Estate. This home has just been totally updated. Includes 2-space garage. It is in Bennington County, and architectural style is Colonial and Federal, zoned residential.
